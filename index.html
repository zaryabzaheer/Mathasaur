Rules To Make The Game

MORE CHALLENGING

  • Players must roll a six to move a game piece out of the starting area, even if they have game pieces already on the track.
  • Players must move their game pieces clockwise along the track until they reach the safety of the finishing area.
  • The first player to get all four of their game pieces into the finishing area wins the game.
  • Once a game piece is on the track, it can move forward the number of spaces shown on the die.
